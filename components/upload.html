Artwork Guidelines

Resolution
Minimum 300 DPI at final print size. Low-resolution files may appear blurry when printed.
Colour Mode
CMYK colour mode for print. RGB files will be converted — slight colour shifts may occur.
Bleed & Safe Zone
Include 3mm bleed on all edges. Keep important content 5mm from trim lines.
Fonts
Outline all fonts or embed them in the file to prevent substitution issues.
Not Sure?
Upload what you have and our team will advise. We offer free pre-press checks on all files.
